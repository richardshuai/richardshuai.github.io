Richard Shuai

I am a current senior at UC Berkeley pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Statistics. I'm currently researching machine learning applications in both protein engineering and genetics.

In Professor Jeffrey Gray's lab at Johns Hopkins, I've been developing deep learning approaches for designing antibody sequences under the mentorship of PhD student Jeffrey Ruffolo. Our work allows us to generate synthetic antibody libraries that can accelerate the development of therapeutic antibodies.
